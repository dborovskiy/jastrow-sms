Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5/26/2025

Jastrow SMS Lookup is a personal dictionary lookup service that allows users to send Hebrew or Aramaic words by SMS or keypad entry and receive dictionary lookup results.

Information We Collect

When you use this service, we may collect:
- Your phone number
- The SMS messages or keypad entries you send
- The dictionary lookup results generated in response
- Basic technical logs needed to operate and debug the service

How We Use Information

We use this information only to:
- Receive your lookup request
- Process the requested dictionary lookup
- Send you the requested response
- Operate, troubleshoot, and improve the service

SMS Messaging

By opting in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Jastrow SMS Lookup containing dictionary lookup results in response to your requests.

Message frequency varies based on your usage. Message and data rates may apply. You may reply STOP to opt out. You may reply HELP for help.

No Marketing Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose mobile phone numbers, opt-in data, or SMS messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Third-Party Services

This service may use third-party providers such as Twilio for SMS delivery, Render for hosting, and Sefaria for dictionary lookup data. These providers are used only to operate the service.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate, troubleshoot, and improve the service.
